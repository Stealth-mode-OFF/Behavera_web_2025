Pricing questions we hear most

How is the price calculated?

Starter runs on a per-active-employee licence. Growth and Enterprise combine employee tiers with modules you activate. We tailor discounts for 1,000+ employees.

Can we start small?

Yes. Begin with a 30-day pilot for 10–25 people, validate insights, and then expand when you have the business case.

What languages and security standards do you support?

Behavera supports EN/CZ/SK and follows SOC2-aligned security practices with custom DPA and SSO for Enterprise.

Do you integrate with HRIS or BI tools?

Yes. Growth and Enterprise include API + webhooks and native exports for BambooHR, Personio, and PowerBI.
